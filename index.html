About me

I am a podiatrist by training and have spent the past decade exploring various healthcare settings within Singapore, at different parts of the patient care continuum.

I joined an apprenticeship program as part of a mid-career switch, which transformed me from being code-illiterate to code-literate, and ignited a passion for data, coding and technology.

I'm also an avid traveller and have trekked through remote villages in the Himalayas.
